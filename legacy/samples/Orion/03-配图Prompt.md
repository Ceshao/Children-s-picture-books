# Orion · 配图 Prompt（可直接复制进 ChatGPT 生图）

> 用法：先出「第 0 步」角色参考图，定稿后每页都**附上参考图** + 复制对应 prompt。
> 每条 prompt 都已经把【风格锚点块】+【角色锚点块】拼好，直接用。

---

## 第 0 步 · 先出角色参考图（最重要，定全书一致性）

```
Character reference sheet, plain soft background, front view and 3/4 view.
STYLE: classic painterly children's-book watercolor, soft warm golden light, lush layered depth, gentle cozy and slightly magical, never scary.
PALETTE: warm amber, sage green, soft sky blue, cream, touches of crystal purple.

ORION: a round-faced young Asian boy about 3 years old, orange hooded jacket, blue star-patterned bucket hat, small crossbody shoulder bag, blue jeans, navy sneakers, black hair, curious gentle face.

PIP: a small friendly baby pterosaur, green body with a cream belly, orange wing membranes, a pointed orange head-crest, big blue eyes, half a head shorter than the boy.
```
→ 选一张满意的存为**主参考图**，后面每页都附它。

---

> **🔒 统一尺寸**：所有页（封面/扉页/内页/满版高潮）都用**同一画布：竖版 2:3（≈1024×1536）**。下面固定块已含 `CANVAS` 行；满版页靠构图铺满同一画布，不换横版。

## 固定块（下面每页都已含，无需另加）

```
STYLE: classic painterly children's-book watercolor, soft warm golden light, lush layered depth, gentle cozy and slightly magical, never scary.
PALETTE: warm amber, sage green, soft sky blue, cream, touches of crystal purple.
CANVAS: portrait 2:3 (≈1024×1536) — the SAME size on every page, cover included.
ORION: round-faced young Asian boy ~3, orange hooded jacket, blue star bucket hat, crossbody bag, blue jeans, navy sneakers — same face & outfit every page.
PIP: small baby pterosaur, green body, cream belly, orange wings, orange head-crest, big blue eyes, shorter than Orion — same every page.
```

## 逐页 Prompt

### 扉页（DET · 羽毛 · 无人物）
```
[固定块]
SHOT: detail still-life, a single soft feather floating in the air, close-up.
SCENE: blurred warm river light in the background, a few sparkles.
ACTION: no characters — just the feather drifting down.
COMPOSITION: feather drifts from upper-left toward lower-right, lots of gentle empty space.
Children's picture book illustration, single page.
```

### P1（EST · 山谷全景 · 无人物或人物极小）
```
[固定块]
SHOT: wide establishing shot, high angle, show the whole secret valley.
SCENE: a sparkling river, a tall waterfall with a faint rainbow, lush ferns, a few distant pterosaur silhouettes in the morning sky.
ACTION: no main characters in frame (or tiny in the far distance) — a calm, inviting empty valley at sunrise.
COMPOSITION: river leads the eye from foreground toward the waterfall, warm morning light.
Children's picture book illustration, single page.
```

### P2（MED · 河边相遇）
```
[固定块]
SHOT: medium shot.
SCENE: the riverbank, a feather just landing, sparkling water.
ACTION: Orion crouches and meets Pip; Pip opens his mouth happily to say hello.
COMPOSITION: the two face each other, warm friendly mood.
Children's picture book illustration, single page.
```

### P3（DET · 发光蛋 · 无人物/仅手入镜）
```
[固定块]

SCENE: a faint shimmering "sky map" of stars glowing beside the nest.
ACTION: no full characters — maybe just a small hand at the edge of frame; focus entirely on the warm glowing egg.
COMPOSITION: egg centered, gentle golden glow, soft dark surroundings to make it pop.
Children's picture book illustration, single page.
```

## 出图后一致性校验
- [ ] **每页尺寸完全一致**（同一竖版 2:3 画布，封面也一样；满版页没换横版）？
- [ ] 每页 Orion 的橙外套/蓝渔夫帽/挎包是否一致？
- [ ] 每页 Pip 的绿身/橙翼/橙冠/大蓝眼是否一致？
- [ ] 暖光水彩画风、色板是否全书统一？
- [ ] **把封面和 P12 并排**——像不像同一个 Orion、同一个 Pip？
- [ ] 非人物页（扉页/P1/P3/P6）有没有不小心又画上主角？
